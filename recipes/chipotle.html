Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Description

This chicken burrito bowl, is a nutritious, delicious and filling meal. It’s a healthier (and less expensive) chipotle copycat. Plus it’s just as tasty (if not more so!). Great for meal prep and easy lunches.

This burrito bowl has it all! Wonderfully flavored chili chicken, a tangy salsa, creamy avocados, sweet corn and much much more! Easy to whip up ahead of time, it’s hearty, delicious while still feeling lovely and fresh.

  • Brown Rice
  • Black Beans
  • Chicken
  • Corn Salsa
  • Lettuce
  1. Cook the brown rice
  2. Simmer the black beans
  3. Cook chicken on a cast iron skillet
  4. Mix corn, jalapenos, cilantro, and lime juice to create salsa
  5. Top with lettuce
